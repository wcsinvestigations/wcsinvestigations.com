About WCS Special Investigations

WCS Special Investigations is a licensed private investigation agency (Florida #A3600029 | Alabama #93) dedicated to supporting criminal defense attorneys and their clients with thorough, street-level investigative work.

Our mission is simple: deliver the factual foundation that helps build the strongest possible defense from day one. We specialize in locating witnesses, conducting recorded interviews, gathering critical evidence, and performing comprehensive background checks — all with the goal of helping defense counsel develop powerful theories of defense, strengthen motions, and achieve better outcomes in plea negotiations and at trial.

Proper investigation at the outset protects not only the client’s freedom but also the attorney from later claims of ineffective assistance of counsel under the Strickland standard and Florida Rule 3.850. Our detailed, professional reports create a clear record of reasonable professional judgment.

We proudly serve private-paying clients who can retain us directly for immediate work, as well as court-appointed and indigent cases through approved funding channels including CJA (federal), JAC (Florida), and OIDS (Alabama).
